About Me


Basically I'm a programmer at heart but an electronics engineer by education. I decide to go in Academia to get the best of these two worlds!


I'm Indonesian. I was born in Java island. I spent my childhood in a city called "Malang". Then I moved to "Surabaya" for high school. Afterwards, I continue my study as an undergraduate student at "Sepuluh November Institute of Technology", majoring in Electrical Engineering. I got the B.Eng (or S.T. in Indonesian) in 2001. In 2006 I started my master degree at the University of Bremen, and got the M.Sc in 2008. In 2011, I started my PhD at the Technical University of Munich (TUM), and got the final degree in 2015. Both my M.Sc and PhD are in the field of Automation and Information engineering. However, my master thesis as well as my PhD dissertation were very much related with the application of neuroscience in the domain of cyber physical systems, including robotics. I did a postdoc research at the University of Manchester (UoM), right after finishing my PhD. After completing my postdoc project at UoM, I returned back to Indonesia and continue working as an associate professor at the Department of Electrical Engineering in Petra Christian University.
